About Sarah

I am a software developer from Akron, Ohio. I design and build on web applications. I am also teach Python to students of all ages at Coding with Kids.

Throughout my coding journey I've accomplished several things including:

  • wrote blog posts and articles about various technical concepts, careers, and freelancing.
  • mentored and empowerd new developers just getting started in tech.
  • managed the online community Elphas Can Code on Elpha.
  • created the Disney Codes Challenge, a coding challenge designed to help developers begin a habit of coding more projects.
  • collaborated with other developers in the tech community to create resources that help others reach their coding goals.

In addition to my tech experience, I taught students academic skills at different schools in Stark County, Ohio and at Sylvan Learning for 9 years. I designed and differentiated curriculum to fit individual learning styles and regularly assessing student progress.
