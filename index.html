About Sarah

I build simple yet current websites in Canton, Ohio. My journey to tech starts with a degree in Education and learning the best strategies to connect with students. When I began the blog The Original BritishPandaChick, I discovered a way to combine my teaching skills with my love for writing, research, and tech. As soon as I discovered coding, I found a way to combine everything I love into a career I love.

Learning how to code has opened my eyes to a brand new world and started me on a journey that allows me the chance to learn something new every day. I made the switch from teaching to front-end development which allows me the opportunity to share what I learn with others and help people find the same enthusiasm I found when I discovered tech.
