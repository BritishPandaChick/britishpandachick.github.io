Hello World! I'm Sarah.

I’m a software engineer and online coding instructor at Coding with Kids, where I teach kids computer science fundamentals through building games in Python. I’ve spoken at virtual conferences like Moms Can Code and Code & Coffe and thoughtfully shared my journey and tips on DEV. I’m now aiming to bring this blend of education, craftsmanship, and user‑centered problem-solving to a software engineering role where I can both build impactful products and empower teammates through collaboration and mentorship.
