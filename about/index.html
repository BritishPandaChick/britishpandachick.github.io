About Sarah

Sarah Bartley is a self taught front-end web developer and content creator at BritishPandaChick. She is best known for her blogs The Original BritishPandaChick, Needlework Kits, and BritishPandaChick Codes. Before she was blogging or coding websites, she taught reading, math, and study skills to students.

Sarah has been coding for the past three years. She builds WordPress websites for local businesss, creatives, and solopreneurs. In 2018, she was recognized for the Grow with Google scholarship. She recently spoke at Moms Can Code's first virtual conference in April and has been featured on Skillcrush sharing her tech story.

When she's not coding or blogging, Sarah can be found making needlecraft kits for Needlework Kits. She particularly likes to do felt applique, cross stitch, and embroidery. She is currently learning how to crochet in her spare time. Sarah also enjoys reading, watching cooking shows, and playing mobile games.
