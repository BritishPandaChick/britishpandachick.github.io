About Sarah

Sarah Bartley is a front-end web developer and content creator at BritishPandaChick. She consult and build WordPress websites with small businesses, creatives, and solopreneurs. Before she was creating web applications, she taught reading, math, and study skills to students.

In addition to creating websites, Sarah writes posts for blogs such as BritishPandaChick Codes and Needlework Kits. She began blogging in March 2015 with her first blog The Original BritishPandaChick where she created the My Coding Journey series which shared everything she’s been learned about coding. Sarah contributed posts other platforms including The Practical Dev, App Academy, and Book Prescriber.

When Sarah isn’t coding or blogging, she can be found making needlecraft kits or working on other craft projects. She particularly likes to make felt applique, cross stitch, and embroidery. She also enjoys reading, watching cooking shows, and playing mobile games.
